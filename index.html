The development of Canada Team

text

From 1924 to 2014, Canada team has achieved overall 625 medals. Canada Team started with 9 medals in 1924 Olympic, raised to 21 medals(17 silver, 3 gold, and 1 bronze ) in 1960. From 1964 till 1984 was the decreasing of the amount of medal achieved with 7 medals in 1964, 1 medal in 1972, 2 medals in 1980 and 4 medals in 1984.However, Canada team got a breakthrough from 1994 to 2010, the number of medals achieved increased rapidly from 252 medals to 535 medals. In more specific,the number of Bronze, Silver, Gold medals has achieved are 107, 203, 315, and Canada Team reached the highest position for overall Gold medals on the world- count to 2014. Obviously, Canada Teams are winning.
